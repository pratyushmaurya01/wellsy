# Learning Journey

## Introduction

I am a Computer Science student who started programming during the first year of college after receiving my first laptop. Before that, I spent time improving my typing skills using a tablet and external keyboard. My journey into software development began with curiosity, logical thinking, mathematics, and the desire to build a strong career through technology.

Computer Science attracted me because it combined problem-solving, mathematics, analytical thinking, and strong career opportunities. The field seemed intellectually challenging while also offering excellent growth and financial prospects.

# Hackathon Experience

I participated in a 24-hour hackathon.

One of my biggest mistakes was choosing a cybersecurity problem statement simply because fewer people selected it.

I lacked sufficient domain knowledge in cybersecurity, which made execution difficult.

This experience taught me an important lesson:

Choose problems based on understanding and capability, not on perceived competition.
